Delete Your TowerLite Account

How to permanently delete your account and associated data

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

Step 1: Open the TowerLite app
Step 2: Go to Profile tab (bottom navigation)
Step 3: Tap the Settings icon (top right)
Step 4: Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
Step 5: Confirm deletion when prompted

What Data Gets Deleted

  • User Profile: Username, email, profile picture, phone number
  • Reports: All power status reports you've posted
  • Comments: All comments you've made on reports
  • Achievements: All earned achievements and points
  • Saved Locations: All locations you've saved
  • Activity History: All likes, interactions, and activity
  • Push Tokens: Notification preferences and tokens

Once you confirm deletion in the app:

  • Your account is scheduled for permanent deletion. You will be logged out, and your profile and content will no longer be visible to other users.
  • We provide a **7-day recovery period** in case you change your mind or if the deletion was a mistake.
  • After the 7-day period, all of your data listed above will be **permanently and irreversibly deleted** from our systems.
